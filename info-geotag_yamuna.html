This module is for geotagging photos associated with monsoonal floods from New Delhi’s Yamuna River. Local knowledge of New Delhi is useful for this module.
If you can only give a rough estimate of where a photo is, that is still valuable. Just circle the area you think and move onto the next one.
If it is not possible to geotag the photo, don't worry! Just click ‘don't know’ then save your answer, it is still valid and useful.
